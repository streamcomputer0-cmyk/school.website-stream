CBSE Curriculum

FOUNDATIONAL STAGE CURRICULUM

At Sarvottam, we think that education for young minds is a fascinating journey packed with various and engaging learning routes spread across five levels, known as the Fantastic Five. We acknowledge and celebrate each child's unique talents. Our curriculum incorporates internationally recognised educational philosophies, which include:

Reggio Emilia: Hands-on learning opportunities allow kids to find themselves and grow holistically.

High Scope: Children actively shape their knowledge via exploration, inquiry, and interaction with their environment.

Montessori: Learners direct their own progress in a collaborative and carefully planned environment through independent and meaningful engagement.

Waldorf: This approach develops the child by combining cognition, emotion, and action via an academic and arts-rich curriculum.

PREPARATORY STAGE CURRICULUM

The primary school curriculum is purposefully developed to nurture curiosity and creative thinking. It blends proven teaching methods with dynamic learning experiences, laying a strong foundation for future success. Alongside the core language curriculum, our Language Enrichment Program enhances communication through public speaking and presentations. Supported by interactive co-learning spaces, a robotics lab, a 5,500 sq. ft. double-height library with separate digital and reading zones, and an open-air theatre, the curriculum offers students varied platforms to express and excel, skills essential for the 21st century.
